Frequently Asked Questions

What is SerenityCare AI?

SerenityCare AI is an intelligent assistant designed to provide immediate mental health support and information. It's available 24/7 through WhatsApp to help you whenever you need it most.

Is it free?

Yes, SerenityCare AI is completely free to use. Our goal is to make mental health support accessible to everyone in Nigeria.

How do I start?

Simply scan the QR code on this page or click the "Start Conversation" button to begin chatting with SerenityCare AI on WhatsApp. You can also save our number +234 702 674 3998 to your contacts and text us directly.

Is my information private?

Absolutely. All conversations with SerenityCare AI are confidential. We do not store personal information or conversation history. Your privacy and trust are our highest priorities.

Can AI replace therapy?

No, SerenityCare AI is not a replacement for professional therapy or medical treatment. It's a supplementary tool designed to provide immediate support and connect you with resources. For serious mental health concerns, please contact Serenity Royale Hospital directly.
